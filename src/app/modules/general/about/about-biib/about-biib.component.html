BIIB – A Launch Pad For New Ideas and Dreams!

BIIB - Balaji Institute of International Business has always been a launch pad for new ideas and innovative solutions. BIIB is the place where curious minds blend to make a difference. Our goal is to expand our boundaries by creating new pathways for knowledge and empowering the world with enlightened people. Balaji Institute of International Business (BIIB), is an ‘A++’ Grade’ AICTE accredited Business School with an outstanding placement record. Established in 2003, we are the fastest-growing B-Schools in India. Our most advanced training and scientific grooming have resulted in a rich corporate career for thousands of students pursuing successful corporate careers globally.

Why BIIB

Our unmatched teaching methodology and curriculum is based on international management training philosophy. Our student-friendly teaching staff is highly qualified and professionally experienced to mentor aspiring students. Today, BIIB boasts of the most progressive pedagogy and a 100% placement rate.

Learning beyond classrooms not only gives hands-on experience but builds your self-confidence to face the challenges of the corporate world. The cultural and co-curricular activities develop a strong instinct to lead and win competitions.

An uncompromised environment with everything you need is thoughtfully created for uninterrupted attention. Here, you will experience uncongested life with facilities and amenities second to none. At BIIB you will cherish moments that will remain with you for a lifetime.
